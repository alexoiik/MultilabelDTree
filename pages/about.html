About the Project
The MultilabelDTree app was developed by Alexandros Oikonomou, an undergraduate student in the department of Information and Electronic Engineering (IEE) at the International Hellenic University (IHU).

This application was created as part of his final BSc thesis, entitled "MultilabelDTree: An Automated Machine Learning Web Application for Classifying Multi-Label Data through Decision Trees", under the supervision of Assistant Professor Mr. Stefanos Ougiaroglou.
